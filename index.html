I grew up in Northeastern Illinois and Fresno, California. After receiving my B.S. in Mechanical Engineering and B.A. in Mathematics from the State University of New York (SUNY) at Buffalo, I worked full time for a few years before enrolling in Old Dominion University to pursue a M.S. in Computer Science.

My main interest in the modeling and implementation of dynamic systems. I intend to use my knowledge of dynamics and control, as well as my experience with designing embedded systems to advance the field of automation. Ideally, I would like to work on technologies related to workforce replacement, in order to make the workplace environment safer and more productive.
